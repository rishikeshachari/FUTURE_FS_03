Our Story

A Decade of Trust & Excellence in Anantapur

Founded by Shri Sathyanarayana, our family has been serving the people of Anantapur for over a decade. What started as a small workshop in Old Town has grown into a trusted name in hallmarked jewellery.

We believe jewellery is not just an ornament – it's an emotion, an investment, and a legacy. Every piece that leaves our store carries our promise of purity, craftsmanship, and fair pricing.
